Senior Data Scientist Position

Company: DataTech Analytics
Location: San Francisco, CA (Remote friendly)
Department: Data Science & Analytics

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Senior Data Scientist to join our analytics team. This role focuses on building machine learning models, conducting advanced statistical analysis, and driving data-driven insights for business decisions.

Requirements:
- 4+ years of experience in data science and machine learning
- Expert knowledge of Python data science stack (pandas, numpy, scikit-learn)
- Strong experience with machine learning frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch, Keras)
- Proficiency in statistical analysis and experimental design
- Experience with data visualization tools (matplotlib, seaborn, plotly)
- Knowledge of SQL and database management systems
- Experience with cloud platforms for ML (AWS SageMaker, Azure ML, GCP)
- Understanding of MLOps and model deployment pipelines
- Strong mathematical and statistical background
- Experience with A/B testing and causal inference

Responsibilities:
- Develop and deploy machine learning models for business applications
- Conduct statistical analysis and hypothesis testing
- Build predictive models and recommendation systems
- Collaborate with engineering teams on model deployment
- Create data visualizations and dashboards for stakeholders
- Design and analyze A/B tests and experiments
- Research and implement new ML algorithms and techniques
- Mentor junior data scientists and analysts
- Present findings to technical and non-technical audiences

Nice to Have:
- PhD in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, or related field
- Experience with deep learning and neural networks
- Knowledge of natural language processing (NLP) techniques
- Experience with time series forecasting
- Familiarity with big data technologies (Spark, Hadoop)
- Experience with containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Knowledge of data engineering pipelines
- Experience with real-time ML systems

Benefits:
- Competitive salary with equity and performance bonuses
- Comprehensive health and wellness benefits
- Flexible work arrangements and unlimited PTO
- Professional development budget for conferences and courses
- Access to cutting-edge hardware and cloud computing resources
- Collaborative data science team environment
- Opportunity to work on innovative ML projects

This position offers an excellent opportunity to work on challenging data science problems and shape the future of analytics at DataTech Analytics.
